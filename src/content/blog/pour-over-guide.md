A great pour over starts with freshly ground beans and a little patience. At Fourth Coffee, we believe the pour over is the purest way to experience a single-origin coffee. No machine between you and the flavor. It's a ritual that rewards attention to detail, and once you nail the technique, you'll never look at drip coffee the same way.

The key is controlling your pour. A slow, steady spiral from the center outward ensures even extraction. Rush it, and you'll get a thin, sour cup. Take your time, and you'll unlock caramel sweetness and bright, complex acidity that makes specialty coffee worth the effort.

## What You'll Need
- Fresh coffee
- Gooseneck kettle
- Pour over dripper

## The Method
1. Heat water to 205°F
2. Rinse the filter
3. Add 22g coffee
4. Bloom for 30 seconds
5. Pour slowly

Enjoy! ☕

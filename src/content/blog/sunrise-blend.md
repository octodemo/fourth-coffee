We're excited to announce **Sunrise Blend**, our newest seasonal offering crafted for those early morning moments when you need something bright, warm, and uplifting. Our roasters spent three months dialing in this blend, combining beans from Ethiopia and Guatemala to create something truly special.

Sunrise Blend was inspired by the golden light that hits the roastery each morning through our east-facing windows. We wanted a cup that captured that feeling: optimistic, energizing, and impossible not to smile through. Whether you brew it as espresso or filter, the citrus notes shine through beautifully.

## Tasting Notes
- Citrus and honey aroma
- Caramel and orange flavor
- Medium, silky smooth body
